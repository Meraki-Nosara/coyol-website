### Key Findings

#### ✅ La Luna — Excellent Performance
- **46% profit margin** — well above industry standard (10-15%)
- Food cost at 38% — slightly high but acceptable for fine dining
- Labor at 16% — very efficient staffing
- **La Luna generates 93% of total group profit**

#### ⚠️ Coyol — High Food Costs
- **63% food cost** vs industry target of 28-35%
- Labor is reasonable at 19%
- **Recommendation:** Review supplier pricing, portion sizes, and menu prices

#### 🔴 Esh — Operating at Loss
- **50% labor cost** — way above 25-35% target
- Sales volume (₡22M) may not justify current staffing
- **Recommendation:** Reduce hours or share staff with Coyol

### Recommendations

1. **Coyol — Reduce Food Cost**

2. **Esh — Reduce Labor Cost**

   - Consider sharing staff with Coyol during slow hours
   - Target: Reduce from 50% to 30%

3. **La Luna — Maintain Excellence**
   - Continue current operations
   - Consider if any practices can transfer to other locations
