# 🌴 Coyol Restaurant — Nosara, Costa Rica

## Overview

**Coyol** is a beloved farm-to-table restaurant located in the heart of Nosara, Guanacaste Province, Costa Rica. Known for its commitment to locally-sourced ingredients and contemporary Costa Rican cuisine, Coyol has become a culinary destination for both locals and tourists visiting this surf and yoga paradise.

---

## 📍 Location & Atmosphere

- **Address:** Playa Guiones, Nosara, Guanacaste, Costa Rica
- **Setting:** Open-air tropical dining with lush garden surroundings
- **Vibe:** Casual elegance meets jungle chic — perfect for sunset dinners
- **Capacity:** Intimate setting, reservations recommended during peak season

---

## 🍽️ Cuisine & Menu Highlights

**Style:** Modern Costa Rican / Farm-to-Table / Fusion

### Signature Dishes:
- **Ceviche Tico** — Fresh catch of the day with local citrus, cilantro, and ají
- **Grilled Octopus** — Tender octopus with chimichurri and roasted vegetables
- **Casado Reinventado** — A modern twist on the classic Costa Rican casado
- **Plantain Gnocchi** — House-made gnocchi with local cheese and herbs
- **Chocolate Lava Cake** — Made with Nosara-grown cacao

### Beverages:
- Craft cocktails featuring local fruits and herbs
- Curated wine list with South American selections
- Fresh-pressed tropical juices
- Local craft beers
