# Mar Azul — Municipal Meeting Summary

**Date:** April 2026  
**Location:** Municipalidad de Nicoya  
**Attendees:** Marion Peri, Architect (project representative), Municipal Advisor to Mayor Gerardo Camacho

---

## Background

Mar Azul is a legally established condominium with:
- All SETENA permits and active bitácora
- Municipal construction permits (permisos de construcción)
- Own water wells with full documentation (perforation permits, water quality, MINAE concession)
- Private electrical line since 2014

The project has operated for 25 years with good municipal relations until recent months.

---

## Current Situation

### The Suspension

César Espinoza (Municipal Construction Engineer) **suspended obras civiles** based on an old MINAE office letter (May 2025) noting incomplete electrical installation at a well — which has since been fully completed and documented.

**His stated reason:** "No water supply"

**Reality:** 
- Project has its own legally permitted wells
- Additionally donated a well to ASADA Santa Marta
- 450 residents of Liberia Norte now have potable water thanks to this donation
- All documentation submitted to municipal platform

### Pattern of Conduct

1. **Inconsistent decisions:** Same uso de suelos document accepted for main house construction, rejected months later for guest house on same property

2. **Improper inspections:** Entering private condominium property accompanied by complainants and their lawyers (SFERA Legal), without coordinating with administration

3. **Escalating threats:** After 3 pages about water issues, latest official letter adds: "This municipality will also investigate your electrical availability"

### The Complainants

- Residents of Lots 1, 4, 20, 32 (hostile neighbours)
- Represented by SFERA Legal / Roberto Castillo
- Have filed multiple failed lawsuits (trees, earth movement, administrative fraud)
- Currently facing cobros judiciales for unpaid HOA fees
- Appear to coordinate with César for inspections

---

## Meeting Outcomes

### Municipality Acknowledged:

1. Mar Azul is "a known topic" being discussed internally
2. They want developers to succeed when complying with regulations
3. The situation is complex due to pressure from both sides
4. **César is currently under a disciplinary procedure** — limiting their ability to discuss him

### Municipality Committed To:

1. **Schedule a meeting** with Legal department and technical staff
2. **Review documentation** — if technical requirements are met, should be able to proceed
3. **Separate technical issues from legal disputes** with neighbours

### Key Statement from Municipal Advisor:

> "If there's no technical problem, you should be able to move forward. The legal issues are separate from the technical approvals."
