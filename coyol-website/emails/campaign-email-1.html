You know us from the table.

For over 20 years, La Luna, Coyol, and Esh have welcomed guests to Nosara. What started as a single restaurant on the beach became three gathering places woven into the fabric of this community.

Now we're building something new in the hills above town.

Mar Azul is a private community of homesites and residences set within the jungle canopy, with views stretching to the Pacific. It's the same philosophy that shaped our restaurants: thoughtful design, natural materials, and spaces that bring people together.

If you've ever dreamed of a place in Costa Rica, we'd love to share what we're creating.
