You know us from the table.

Twenty-three years ago, La Luna opened on the beach in Nosara — a simple restaurant with good food and an open kitchen. Back then, the road was still dirt and the town was just a handful of surfers and fishermen. People came for the waves and stayed for dinner.

Over the years, we added Coyol in the hills and Esh by the river. Three restaurants, three different moods, but the same idea: create spaces where people feel at home. Where a meal turns into a conversation, and a conversation turns into a friendship.

Now we're building something new.

Mar Azul is a private community in the jungle above Nosara — homesites and residences with views to the Pacific. The same care we put into our restaurants, we're putting into these homes: natural materials, thoughtful design, spaces that breathe.

If you've ever thought about a place in Costa Rica, we'd love to show you what we're creating.
